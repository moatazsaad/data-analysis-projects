## Tableau Dashboard Project – European Bike Sales (Kaggle Dataset)

[View Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/moataz.osama/viz/BikeSalesAnalysis_17098231966970/SalesDashboard)

- Designed an interactive Tableau dashboard to visualize European bike sales data, improving insights into product performance and customer demographics.
- Created a Top 10 Products by Sales horizontal bar chart using sets and filters to highlight high-demand items based on order quantity.
- Developed a Profit by Subcategory bar chart, segmented by gender, to analyze profit distribution and customer behavior.
- Built a Bubble Chart displaying revenue by age group and product subcategory, enabling multi-metric visualization for trend identification.
- Implemented a Profit by Country Map with dynamic circle sizing to compare geographic performance.
- Added KPI cards for total profit and Year-over-Year % Change, using parameters and calculated fields to support comparative analysis.
- Applied consistent formatting, custom color palettes, and user-friendly interactivity to enhance dashboard readability and usability.
