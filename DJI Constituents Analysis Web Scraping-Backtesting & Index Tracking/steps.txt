### **Project Workflow: Data Collection and Strategy Backtesting**  

1. **Scraping Dow Jones Constituents**  
   - Extracted the Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJI) constituents table from Wikipedia.  
   - The table includes **30 companies** with details such as **company name, ticker, and date added**.  
   - Saved the data as **const.csv** (without index).  

2. **Downloading Historical Data**  
   - **DJI Index Data (2007–2020):** Retrieved historical DJI index data, including open and close prices.  
   - Saved as **dji.csv**.  
   - **Constituent Stock Prices (2007–2020):** Downloaded daily price data for **180 tickers** using Yahoo Finance (yfinance).  
   - Saved as **const_prices.csv**.  

3. **Data Preparation**  
   - Created a DataFrame with **dates as index** and DJI **closing prices**.  
   - Filtered data for **2010–2020** to focus on relevant trends.  

4. **Strategy Development & Backtesting**  
   - **Simple Momentum Strategy:**  
     - Added **DJI return** and **position** columns.  
     - Calculated **strategy returns** and plotted DJI close vs. momentum strategy.  
     - Analyzed return and risk.  

   - **Simple Contrarian Strategy:**  
     - Plotted DJI close vs. contrarian strategy.  

   - **Modified Profitable Strategy:**  
     - Implemented a strategy that sells if profit exceeds **0.01**.  
     - Visualized performance against DJI close.  

   - **Moving Average Strategy:**  
     - Plotted **50-day SMA** vs. **DJI close**.  
     - Added **200-day SMA** and analyzed **crossovers**:  
       - **+1** when **SMA50 > SMA200**  
       - **-1** when **SMA50 < SMA200**  
     - Plotted strategy performance.  
     - Evaluated its contrarian version.  

   - **Perfect Prediction Strategy:**  
     - Simulated an ideal scenario where returns are predicted in the morning to define positions accordingly.  
     - Plotted results to compare against real-world strategies.
